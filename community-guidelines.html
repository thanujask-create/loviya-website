Community Guidelines

Last updated: May 2026

1. Be Real

  • Use your real identity and truthful profile information.
  • Do not use fake, stolen, celebrity, AI-generated, or misleading photos.
  • Do not pretend to be another person.

2. Be Respectful

  • No harassment, bullying, threats, insults, or hate speech.
  • Respect culture, religion, nationality, gender, language, and personal boundaries.
  • No repeated unwanted messages after someone says no or stops replying.

3. Keep LOVIYA Safe

  • No scams, fraud, blackmail, money requests, crypto promotion, or fake investment offers.
  • No prostitution, trafficking, illegal services, or sexual exploitation.
  • No sending or requesting private documents, bank details, or passwords.

4. Photos & Content

  • No nudity, sexual acts, violent content, hate symbols, drugs, or offensive content.
  • No photos of minors as profile photos.
  • No commercial advertisements, spam, links, or promotional content.

5. Chat Rules

  • Be polite and respectful in messages.
  • Do not send explicit images or sexual messages without consent.
  • Do not pressure users for money, meetings, photos, travel, visas, or personal information.

6. Meeting Safety

  • Meet in public places.
  • Tell a trusted person before meeting someone.
  • Be careful if someone quickly asks to move outside LOVIYA to WhatsApp, Viber, Line, Telegram, or other apps.
  • Never send money or private documents to someone you meet online.

7. Reporting & Blocking

  • Use report and block tools when you see unsafe behavior.
  • False reports or abuse of the reporting system may lead to account action.
  • LOVIYA may review reports, messages, photos, verification status, and account safety signals.

8. Account Action

LOVIYA may warn, restrict, pause, suspend, deactivate, or delete accounts that break these guidelines or create risk for other users.
