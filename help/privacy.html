Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as email, login method, user ID, and account status.
  • Profile details such as name, gender, birthday, country, city, photos, bio, preferences, and verification status.
  • Interaction data such as likes, visits, matches, passes, blocks, reports, and support tickets.
  • Chat and message data needed for messaging, safety review, moderation, and support.
  • Device, notification token, app version, and technical information needed for app operation.

2. Location & Discovery Data

  • LOVIYA may use country, city, district, or approximate location information to improve matching and discovery.
  • Users should not share exact private addresses in profiles or chats.
  • Location controls may be improved over time to give users better privacy options.

3. Verification Data

  • LOVIYA may collect selfies, document images, live face images, and profile photo checks for verification and safety.
  • Verification data may be reviewed by authorized LOVIYA admin or moderation users.
  • Verification helps reduce fake profiles but does not fully guarantee any person’s identity or future behavior.

4. Risk, Safety & Moderation Data

  • LOVIYA may process reports, blocks, repeated complaints, message risk patterns, photo risk signals, and verification results.
  • This information may be used to calculate internal trust or risk levels.
  • Risk scoring is used only to protect users, support moderation, and reduce harmful behavior.

5. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To show profiles, matches, visits, likes, messages, and notifications.
  • To operate verification, moderation, safety, anti-fraud, and support systems.
  • To improve app performance, prevent abuse, and protect users.
  • To comply with legal, security, platform, or store requirements where applicable.

6. Sharing of Information

  • LOVIYA does not sell personal profile data to other users.
  • Some profile information is visible to other users as part of the dating app experience.
  • LOVIYA may share limited data with hosting, storage, analytics, notification, advertising, or security service providers when needed to operate the app.
  • LOVIYA may disclose information if required by law, legal request, safety investigation, or to protect users from serious harm.

7. Advertising & Third-Party Services

  • LOVIYA may use advertising services to support the app.
  • Third-party services may process device or usage information according to their own policies.
  • Users should review device permissions, app permissions, and platform privacy prompts carefully.

8. Data Security

  • LOVIYA takes reasonable technical and organizational steps to protect user data.
  • No internet platform can guarantee 100% security.
  • Users should protect their login email, password, device, and personal information.

9. Data Retention

  • LOVIYA may keep account, verification, message, report, block, moderation, and safety logs while an account is active.
  • Some records may be retained after account deletion when needed for fraud prevention, abuse prevention, dispute handling, safety, or legal compliance.
  • Retention periods may depend on technical, safety, and legal requirements.

10. User Rights & Controls

  • Users may edit profile information from inside the app where allowed.
  • Users may block, report, pause, deactivate, or request account deletion.
  • Users may contact LOVIYA support for account, safety, verification, or privacy requests.

11. International Users

  • LOVIYA may serve users from Sri Lanka, Thailand, Myanmar, and other countries.
  • Data may be stored or processed using international cloud services.
  • Users are responsible for following their local laws when using the app and meeting people.

12. Children & Underage Users

  • LOVIYA is not for users under 18.
  • If LOVIYA discovers underage use, the account may be removed or restricted.
  • Users should report suspected underage accounts immediately.

13. Updates to This Policy

LOVIYA may update this Privacy Policy as the app grows, safety systems change, or legal requirements change.

14. Contact

For privacy, account, or safety requests, please contact LOVIYA through official support channels.
